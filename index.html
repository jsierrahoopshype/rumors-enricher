Presto Helper Bookmarklet

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any Presto story editor page to auto-fill the Slug field.

Presto Auto-Fill
How to install:
  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B / Cmd+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the "Presto Auto-Fill" button above into your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to a Presto story editor page
  4. Click the bookmarklet — it will:
    • Read the Main Headline field and convert it to a lowercase-hyphenated slug
    • Fill the Slug input field with that slug

Slack Helper Bookmarklet

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar. Click it while on a Slack page to open all Enricher and Presto Copy links from the last 10 minutes.

Open All ARAs (Slack)
How to install:
  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B / Cmd+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the "Open All ARAs (Slack)" button above into your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to a Slack channel with Enricher notifications
  4. Click the bookmarklet — it will:
    • Scan visible Slack messages from the last 10 minutes
    • Find all Enricher links (rumors-enricher) and Presto Copy links (presto-suite.gannettdigital.com/copy)
    • Open each link in a new tab
