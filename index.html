Presto Helper Bookmarklet

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any Presto story editor page to auto-fill the Slug and Classification fields.

Presto Auto-Fill
How to install:
  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B / Cmd+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the "Presto Auto-Fill" button above into your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to a Presto story editor page
  4. Click the bookmarklet — it will:
    • Read the page headline and convert it to a lowercase-hyphenated slug
    • Fill the Slug input field with that slug
    • Set the Classification dropdown to sports/nba
